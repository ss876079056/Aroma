About me
Leah Liu is an artist, designer, and social researcher who likes to point out the absurd and thoughtless nature of society while creating something innovative, visually satisfying, and thought-provoking. Her works often satirize consumerism, misogyny, and our post-human technological society because she thinks designers are responsible for questioning society and re-designing the future. As a social researcher, she discovered that the relationship between people and the world around them is created by the design of the products they use. Most commercial designs create an exploitative relationship between people and the planet. Therefore, she wants to design things that can make a sustainable relationship between people and the planet to last for a long time.
